Idea constellations

These pieces are no longer the front door of the project. They now function as conceptual constellations orbiting the central sadhana of noetic sovereignty.

Access note

The root of the project now lives in the main sadhana. These satellite pieces remain here to expand, tension, and contrast it.

Reading note

It would be possible to attempt a complete multidimensional graph of these ideas on the web. But there is no real need for that, at least not yet. This collection uses a simpler solution: constellations of navigable pieces orbiting a center and returning to it when needed.

This is not a deficiency in the project. It is a decision for clarity. For now, what is practicable matters more than what is totalizing.

Relational map · Conceptual navigation

The pieces are not isolated: they describe the same problem at different scales

This project is no longer just a file menu. It is a root infographic: a map showing how need, consciousness, education, species, and energy chain together. Each piece grabs one layer of the problem and pushes it to the next level.

Shared axis

How does an impulse travel from the self to a form of life that does not destroy itself?

The infographics orbit the same question. They begin with need, move through learning and the design of mind, scale up to human coordination, and end at the material dilemma of every civilization: capturing energy, organizing it, and not breaking the support that makes life habitable.

Need JTBD shows that action begins in a real job, not in a market label.
Self-regulation Metacognition shows that learning well requires watching one’s own learning.
Mental form Krishnamurti and the banausos warn against a mind that is useful but broken.
Species scale Panhumanism tries to pull the self out of its myopia and think collective interests.
Externalized memory AI turns language into operational memory and accelerates the loop between idea, critique, and action.
Human rhythm Leisure introduces the limit without which technical acceleration eventually hollows out its user.

Quick reading of the whole set

`JTBD` asks what moves us. `Metacognition` asks how we learn. `Banausos` asks what a narrow education turns us into. `Situated AI` asks what happens when memory and abstraction become conversational and externalized. `Leisure and business` asks what rhythm is required to keep that power from dehumanizing us. `Panhumanism` asks how the self aligns with the species. `Sun, language, and civilization` asks on what material and symbolic basis all this can be sustained. `Technology and historical warning` asks how often that tension has already appeared before. `Noetic sovereignty and the human frontier of AI` asks what perceptual discipline the human needs when the tool starts competing for context and judgment. Nine became ten; one puzzle.

The whole project is static HTML: open it and look. No build, no ceremony, no smoke.
