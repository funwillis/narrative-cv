A Love from Another Planet

10 min Logbook Noetic panhumanism

The narrator meets a woman in a library, loses his footing while reading `The Aleph`, and begins with her a journey that mixes romance, philosophy, and civilizational conspiracy. Plato, Jesus Christ, Augustine, Jung, and Krishnamurti appear as living stations of one shared mutation: moving from compulsion to consciousness, and from consciousness to enough stillness to board the ship toward planet `Love`.
